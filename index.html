Case Studies

A cleaner City one block at a time.

‚ú®500-block Capp

Capp Street is notorious for its litter and odor. Beginning June 2020, Clean Streets activated daily (7 days/week) litter pick-up along the 500 block of Capp Street. Daily attention transforms streets.

‚ú®20th/Capp Trash Can

20th Street near Alioto Park sees considerable foot-traffic. In July 2020, Clean Streets deployed an extra garbage can at this site to provide needed overfill capacity, dramatically reducing litter touching the sidewalk.

FAQ

Isn't this the City's job?

No, the City does not proactively remove litter from sidewalks. The City sweeps roadways and empties public trashcans; residents and business owners clean sidewalks.
