Frequently asked questions

Share these answers when you invite neighbors or businesses to get involved.

How is Clean Streets funded?

We are primarily funded by recurring monthly contributions from residents and merchants, augmented by grants and volunteer sponsorships. Every contribution stays in the neighborhood that raised it.

Which neighborhoods do you serve?

We currently focus on the Mission District and surrounding corridors while piloting programs in additional San Francisco neighborhoods as funding becomes available.

Can I request a cleanup outside of covered blocks?

Yes. Submit a request and we’ll connect you with block captains, cost estimates, and volunteer toolkits to kickstart service on your street.

Do you partner with local government?

We collaborate with city departments and community benefit districts to coordinate services, avoid duplication, and share data that informs long-term infrastructure improvements.
