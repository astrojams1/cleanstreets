Clean Streets

Community supported, socially responsible litter pickup serving the Mission District.

Clean Streets works for the benefit of the Mission. We take no profit and treat our workers and community members right. We hope you consider supporting us!

Become a Patron!

How we serve:

🧹 Litter Pickup

We offer daily, weekly, and custom litter pickup along sidewalks. Today we cover a small region of Central Mission with the goal to serve the entire Mission District by the end of 2021.

🦺 311 Reports

For items we cant move ourselves, we submit and follow up on 311 reports. Follow us on twitter to keep up with our artufl reports.

🗑 Overflow Bins

An overflowing garbage bin on a windy day 💨 will litter an entire block. We provide overflow garbage bins near frequently overflowing public bins to keep garbage contained.
